Tip-Of-The-Week

TOTW from me this week, find a personal project you like and have at it. A personal project always has a little heart behind it and is a great way to learn. Work with a language and/or tools that you have not worked with before. Work in an area you have not worked in before, etc. This learning experience on personal projects has been great to me and has been a valuable tool in the past
