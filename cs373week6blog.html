Tip-Of-The-Week

TOTW is on 3D Printing. After doing some initial research it seemed that there were a few well reviewed 3D printers, however it seemed to show a front running in the self-assembled, open source and budget category, a Prusa I3. Prusa I3 is just a design and there are multiple companies/people building their specific model and some can be found for a great price on eBay or Amazon. All the software included is open source and free as well.
