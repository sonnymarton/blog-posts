Tip-Of-The-Week

TOTW is to push through. College has been one of the greatest and worst experiences of my life. The indescribable fun and great things I have done in the past 4 years can never be replaced. Final Thoughts

This is to wrap up my thoughts on Downing's Object Oriented Programming course. This has been a very difficult course, however I can now consider myself much more capable C++ developer. This class will be one of the most beneficial college courses I have ever taken. It has been one of the most real world experiences I have had in any course in not only the concepts but also the tools we are forced to be used. If you are reading this and considering taking this course, DO IT (however I do not recommend you take it at the same time as Downing's SWE)!
